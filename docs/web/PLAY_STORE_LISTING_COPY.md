## Full description (4000 chars max)

**SCMessenger gives you true digital sovereignty. No servers, no phone numbers, no tracking.**

SCMessenger is a completely decentralized, peer-to-peer (P2P) messaging application designed for absolute privacy and resilience. Whether you are at a crowded festival with no cell service, hiking off-the-grid, or simply seeking a communication tool that respects your privacy, SCMessenger empowers you to communicate directly with those around you.

Instead of routing your private messages through centralized corporate servers, SCMessenger connects directly to the devices of your friends and family using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Local Wi-Fi (mDNS).

### Key Features:

- **True Peer-to-Peer:** Your messages never touch a centralized server. Communication happens device-to-device.
- **Off-the-Grid Mesh:** Send messages even when you don't have cellular data or internet access. SCMessenger routes packets securely through nearby trusted peers.
- **Zero Knowledge:** SCMessenger does not ask for your phone number, email address, or contact list. You generate an anonymous identity locally on your device.
- **End-to-End Encryption:** Every single message is secured using state-of-the-art cryptography (X25519, ChaCha20Poly1305). Only the intended recipient can decrypt and read your messages.
- **Cross-Platform:** Connect and sync seamlessly between Android and iOS devices.
- **Open Source:** Don't trust, verify. Our core protocol and application code are open source and available for independent audit.

### How it works:

1. Generate your secure Identity Profile locally on your device.
2. Exchange Public Keys with a trusted friend (e.g., via QR code or out-of-band sharing).
3. Start chatting! If your friend is nearby, messages are delivered instantly via BLE or secure Wi-Fi connection. If they are out of range, messages wait patiently on your device until a secure P2P path is available.

_SCMessenger is currently in Alpha. Features and mesh networking capabilities are actively being refined._
