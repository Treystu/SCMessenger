# SCMessenger Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

## Overview

SCMessenger is a peer-to-peer encrypted messenger designed with privacy at its core. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Storage

All data — including your contacts, messages, and identity keys — is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not operate centralized servers that store your message content or contact lists.

## No Data Collection

SCMessenger does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with us or any third parties. There are no analytics trackers, advertising identifiers, or telemetry systems embedded in the app.

## End-to-End Encryption

All messages sent through SCMessenger are protected with end-to-end encryption (E2EE). Only the intended recipient can read your messages. No intermediary — including relay servers — can decrypt message contents.

## Bluetooth and Location

SCMessenger uses Bluetooth Low Energy and Wi-Fi Aware for local peer discovery. Android requires location permission for Bluetooth scanning. **Your precise location is never collected, stored, or shared.**

## User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete all locally stored data at any time via **Settings → Reset Data**.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@scmessenger.net
